# Boundary Challenges

- A useful scenario need not involve code, Git, or a terminal.
- Completion can require judgment about a prose artifact, not a green test suite.
- Context should be selected, not silently vacuumed from every open application.
- Reset must explain what is lost and what learner history remains.
- Accessibility is an end-to-end workflow property, not a single control check.
- Adaptation may require confirmation and must honor rejection.
- The model may fail while the learning experience remains trustworthy and resumable.
